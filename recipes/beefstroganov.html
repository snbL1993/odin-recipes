Beef Stroganov

Description

Beef Stroganov with braised boneless short ribs and mushrooms

Ingredients

  • Boneless short rib - 1 Pound
  • Mushrooms 1/2 Pound
  • Onion 1 Large
  • Carrot 2 Large
  • Beef broth 2 Cups
  1. Cook Beef
  2. Cook Vegetables
  3. Add broth
  4. Cook in oven for 3 hours
